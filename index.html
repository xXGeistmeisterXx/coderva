Recipe #1: Fried butter

  • Time: 1 minute
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
butter one stick
brown sugar 3 cups

Step 1: Put your 3 cups of brown sugar on a plate and spread out evenly.

Step 2: take your stick of butter and roll it in the brown sugar until it is thoughly covered.

Step 3: Take your butter and place it ina a pan at medium heat. Cook for one minute; 15 seconds on each side.

Step 4: Enjoy your fried butter!

Recipe #2: Jelly bean salad

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
assorted jelly beans 2 cups
iceberg lettuce 3 cups
maple syrup 2 tbsp

Step 1: Put your favorite flavors of jelly beans and iceberg lettuce into a large bowl.

Step 2: Stir your jelly beans and lettuce until they are well mixed.

Step 3: Pour your maple syrup. in your salad and mix throughly.

Step 4: Chill for 5 minutes then enjoy your jelly bean salad!

Recipe #3: Warm water

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity
Water (bottled or tap) 5 cups
assorted spices

Step 1: Pour your 5 cups of water into a pot

Step 2: Heat the pot on the stove at low heat for 5 minutes

Step 3: Let your water cool for 5 minutes then pour into individual glasses

Step 4: Season to taste and enjoy your warm water!
